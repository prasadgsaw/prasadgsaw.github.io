SQL Murder Mystery

This challenge requires the user to apply their SQL knowledge to solve the fictional murder mystery.

A murder has taken place and the detective needs my help. The detective gave me the crime scene report, but somehow I lost it. The information that I have now is the murder occured sometime on January 15, 2018 in SQL City.

Here is the schema diagram showing the available tables and their relationship with the database:

Let's find out details about the murder from the "crime_scene_report" table filtering the data based on the information I have: crime type, city and date.


Based on the above description, there were 2 witnesses to the murder. Let's investigate the first witness from the "person" table who lives at the last house on "Northwestern Dr".


Let's investigate the second witness whose name is "Annabel" and lives on "Franklin Ave".


Now that we narrowed down information about the witnesses, it's time to check their testimonies. Let's check the testimonies in the "interview" table.

From the first testimony, it is evident that the killer is a Gold member of the gym with membership number starting with "48Z". Also, the killer got into a car with a plate that included "H42W". Second testimony confirms the killer visited the gym on 9th January.

I need to find someone that matches with all this descriptions and get him to the Police Department.

Trying to find the suspect from the "get_fit_now_member" who is a gold member of the gym with the membership number starting with "48Z".


Let's confirm if any of the above two suspects was in the gym on 9th January from the "get_fit_now_check_in" table.


Now that I know the names of the possible suspects, lets try to find more information to narrow down on the killer. As testified by the first witness, killer got into a car with a plate that included "H42W". The license number is in the "drivers_license" table and the names are in the "person" table, so I need to JOIN these tables in order to have information from both the tables together to find the killer.

The killer is...

Jeremy Bowers!

Let's check Jeremy's testimony.


There's a mastermind behind this crime. As testified by Jeremy, the mastermind is a woman around 65" or 67", has red hair and drives a Tesla Model S. Also, she attended the SQL Symphony Concert thrice in December 2017.

Let's find the mastermind by joining "drivers_license" table with "person" table and "facebook_event_checkin" table as the physical details of an individual are in the "drivers_license" table, name and id of an individual is in the "person" table and details regarding the concert are in the "facebook_event_checkin" table.


The mastermind behind the crime is...

Miranda Priestly!
